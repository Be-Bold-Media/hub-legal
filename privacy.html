Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

This privacy policy describes how Be Bold Media Limited ("we", "us", "our") handles data in connection with Be Bold Hub ("the App"), an internal business application.

1. Scope

The App is a private, internal tool used exclusively by Be Bold Media Limited employees. It is not available to the general public.

2. Data We Access

The App connects to QuickBooks Online to access financial data including invoices, bills, expenses, and supplier information. This data is used solely for internal financial management and reporting.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data accessed from QuickBooks Online is stored on our privately hosted servers, protected by authentication and access controls. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose QuickBooks data to any third parties. Data is accessible only to authorised employees of Be Bold Media Limited.

5. Data Retention

QuickBooks data is retained for as long as needed for business operations. Users may request disconnection of the QuickBooks integration at any time via the App's settings.

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact: admin@bebold.media
